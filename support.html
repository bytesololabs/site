Contact

For bug reports, feature requests, billing questions, or general feedback, please email bytesololabs@gmail.com.

We aim to reply within two business days. Including the information below in your first message helps us resolve issues faster.

When reporting a bug, please include:

  • App name and version (e.g. Solitaire One 1.0.0)
  • Device model and operating system version
  • A short description of the problem and steps to reproduce
  • A screenshot or screen recording, if possible

The app version is shown on the Settings screen.

Frequently asked questions

How do I restore my statistics on a new device?

Solitaire One stores statistics and preferences locally on each device. They do not currently sync across devices. Reinstalling the app or clearing its storage will reset your stats.

Are there ads or in-app purchases?

Solitaire One is free and supported by occasional ads. There are no in-app purchases. Ads appear between games, never during play.

Why am I seeing ads in a different language?

Ads are served by the Google AdMob network based on your device region and language. ByteSolo Labs does not control the content of individual ads. If you see an ad that violates Google's policies, please tap the small "info" icon on the ad to report it.

How do I withdraw consent for personalized ads (EEA / UK)?

Open Solitaire One, go to Settings, and tap Privacy options to review or change your consent choices. This option is shown only for users in regions where consent is required.

How do I delete my data?

Because Solitaire One stores all gameplay data locally on your device and does not require an account, you can delete all data by uninstalling the app or clearing its storage in your device settings. For deletion of any analytics or advertising data held by our service providers, see the Privacy Policy.
